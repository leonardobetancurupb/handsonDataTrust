About us

Data Guardian Trust
A Data Trust is a legal entity established to manage and govern data for the benefit of a specific group of individuals, organizations, or society at large. The core idea behind a Data Trust is to provide a trusted framework where data can be managed responsibly, transparently, and fairly, ensuring that the rights and privacy expectations of individuals are respected.
